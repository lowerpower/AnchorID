### How to get listed
We value every contribution. To be added to this list:
1. Generate your AnchorID at [anchorid.net](https://anchorid.net).
2. Submit a Pull Request adding your name and UUID link to the table above.
3. Your attribution will remain permanently linked to this project's history.

*“Code is ephemeral; contribution is permanent.”*
